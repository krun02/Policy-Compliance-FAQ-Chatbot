Incident Response Policy

1. All security incidents must be reported immediately to the security team.
2. HIPAA: Covered entities must have procedures for responding to and mitigating security incidents (HIPAA 164.308(a)(6)).
3. SOC 2: The organization must maintain an incident response plan that includes preparation, detection, containment, eradication, recovery, and lessons learned.
4. NIST SP 800-53 (IR-4): Response procedures must be developed and tested regularly through tabletop or live exercises.
5. All incidents must be documented in an incident response log and retained per data retention policies.
6. Contact lists and escalation paths must be updated quarterly.
