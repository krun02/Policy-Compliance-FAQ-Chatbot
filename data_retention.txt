Data Retention Policy

1. Data shall be retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill legal, regulatory, or business requirements.
2. HIPAA: All patient records must be retained for at least 6 years from the date of creation or the date when it was last in effect.
3. SOC 2: Retention schedules must be documented and enforced for customer data and audit logs.
4. NIST SP 800-53 (AU-11): Organizations must retain audit logs for a defined period to support investigations and meet compliance needs.
5. Secure deletion procedures (e.g., cryptographic erasure) must be applied once data is no longer required.
6. Backups must comply with the retention policy and be regularly validated for integrity.
